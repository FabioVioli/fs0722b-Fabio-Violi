Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberriers, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) likewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brishing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a pairing knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour
